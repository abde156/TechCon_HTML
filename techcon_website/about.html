History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for local developers and has since grown into one of the most influential technology conferences in the world. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of professionals eager to share, learn, and innovate.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals through technology by providing a platform for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and innovation. TechCon supports open-source initiatives, inclusive tech culture, and emerging tech leaders.
